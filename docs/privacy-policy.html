🌊 Drift

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective: April 2026

Drift ("we", "us", "our") is a social connection app built for genuine human connections. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account information:

  • Phone number (for OTP verification)
  • Email address (if you sign up with email)
  • Name, age, bio, city, college/work
  • Profile photos (up to 6)

Activity data:

  • Posts, comments, reactions you create
  • Connections and messages (end-to-end encrypted in transit)
  • Events you create or attend
  • Games played and results
  • Vibe quiz responses (stored locally + profile)

Device data:

  • Push notification token (for alerts)
  • Device type and OS version (for crash reporting)
  • Accelerometer data (Shake-to-Share feature — never stored, processed on-device only)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your Drift account
  • To show you relevant profiles in Discover
  • To deliver messages, notifications, and game invites
  • To calculate vibe compatibility scores
  • To track coins and streaks (gamification)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore, Storage, Realtime Database)
  • Firebase infrastructure is ISO 27001, SOC 1/2/3 certified
  • Profile photos are stored in Firebase Storage with owner-only write access
  • Messages are transmitted over TLS and stored with Firestore security rules
  • OTP verification is handled by Twilio Verify — your phone number is never stored in plain text by Drift

4. Permissions We Request

📷 Camera 🖼 Photos 🔔 Notifications 📳 Vibration
  • Camera: Scan QR codes and take profile photos. Only accessed when you tap the camera button.
  • Photos: Upload profile pictures and post images. We only access photos you explicitly select.
  • Notifications: Send you connection requests, messages, and streak reminders.
  • Vibration: Haptic feedback for Shake-to-Share feature.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Google Firebase — database, auth, storage infrastructure
  • Twilio — OTP SMS delivery only (phone number sent for verification)
  • Daily.co — real-time voice rooms (room tokens, not recordings)
  • Expo — push notification delivery

All third-party services are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • To delete your account and all associated data: go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or email us
  • Messages in active connections are retained for 90 days after account deletion
  • Coin transaction logs are retained for 1 year for fraud prevention

7. Children's Privacy

Drift requires users to be at least 13 years old. Users under 18 have additional profile visibility restrictions. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data via in-app settings
  • Correction: Edit your profile at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data at any time
  • Portability: Export your data on request

For Indian users: this policy complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you in-app before making material changes to this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
